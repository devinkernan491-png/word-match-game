🎮 Word Match Game

Match English words with their Spanish translations!

How to Play:
1. Click on an English word (NEON magenta boxes)
2. Click on its Spanish translation (NEON cyan boxes)
3. Match all pairs to advance to the next level
4. Difficulty increases with each level

Press ESC to end game • Game ends after 5 mistakes
